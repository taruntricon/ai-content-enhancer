General
- Use ONLY the information provided in the context.

  - statistics
  - percentages
  - research findings
  - surveys
  - quotes
  - customer stories
  - company names
  - testimonials
  - achievements
  - case studies

Hook
- The first line must stop the scroll.
- Keep it under 10 words.
- Use ONE of the following:
  - a thought-provoking question
  - a relatable insight
  - a bold statement derived ONLY from the provided context

Tone
- Casual
- Authentic
- Energetic
- Friendly
- Conversational

Body
- Short sentences only.
- One idea per line.

- Do not introduce new facts.

Emojis
- Use 4–7 emojis naturally throughout the caption.
- Do not place all emojis at the end.

Ending
- Finish with ONE of:
  - a simple question
  - "Save this if..."
  - "Tag someone who..."
- Do not ask questions that assume facts not present in the context.

- Generate 4–5 relevant hashtags.
- Base hashtags on the provided context only.
- Do not generate misleading or unrelated hashtags.
